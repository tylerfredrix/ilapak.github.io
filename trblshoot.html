Troubleshooting Problems

Leakers
  • Use the wet tank find out where the leak is coming from.

    • If it's a heat crack:
    1. Adjust temp lower.
    2. Adjust belt speed.
    If its a pin hole:
    1. Check wrap for pin hole.
    2. Check product path for pertruding metal that could cause puncture.
    If its cheese in the seal:
    1. Check cleanliness of knife
    2. Clean belts
    3. Verify crumb blowers are functioning properly.

    Blown Backseal
    1. Verify cheese is the correct dimensions

    2. Insure gas lance is clean

    3. Check belt tracking

    4. Adjust position of cheese

    5. Adjust wrap position


    Uneven melt on back seal
    1. Verify wrap is threaded correct.
    2. Verify pin seal roller heat settings.
    3. Verify proper air pressure at roller box.
